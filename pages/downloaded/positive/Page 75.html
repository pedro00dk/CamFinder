            ULTRA SLIM & STYLISH

            A Mighty Mini

            Carry something small that captures it all. The NX mini is the world’s slimmest and lightest interchangeable lens camera. Now pro performance fits in your pocket.

            ULTRA SLIM & STYLISH

            Premium Vintage Look

            With its solid metal body and leather-like finish, the NX mini combines modern simplicity with a touch of analogue. It also comes in a choice of 5 colours to match your look.

            PERFECT FOR SELFIES

            Flip Up & Wink

            Flip up the 3” screen and your NX mini is on and ready to take the perfect selfie. Touch it or let your wink be its command. In 2 seconds you’ll have a shot worth sharing.

            CAPTURE & SHARE ON THE GO

            Tap to Share

            When it’s time to share your photos, all it takes is a simple tap with PhotoBeam. Tag & Go is powered by NFC and it transfers the image you’re viewing to any compatible smartphone.

            CAPTURE & SHARE ON THE GO

            Group Share

            The easiest way to transfer multiple photos and albums is via NFC with Mobile Link. Select your moments and the camera does the rest, sharing with up to 4 friends.

            CAPTURE & SHARE ON THE GO

            Easy Syncing

            Save photos as you go with Autoshare. The NX mini sends each new photo to your smartphone or tablet via Wi-Fi. It can even assign GPS tags, so every snap has its spot on the map.

            CAPTURE & SHARE ON THE GO

            Remote Control

            Shoot hands-free. Remote Viewfinder Pro lets you adjust your camera settings using your smartphone and then take the shot when you’re ready – not when the timer tells you.

            OUTSTANDING OPTICS

            Brilliant Detail

            The NX mini’s super-sized 20.5 MP 1” sensor delivers an amazingly high resolution that puts smartphones in their place. All those pixels mean stunning detail and true-to-life colours.

            OUTSTANDING OPTICS

            Superior Actions Shots

            The shutter blinks at the increadibly fast speed of 1/16000 second. Now you can catch the decisive moment you want to hold onto, even as your subject flies by.

            More Features

            • No more blur and noise

              Low light is no problem with the NX mini. It shoots up to ISO 25600, making it very sensitive to light, and it boasts an optical image stabiliser. That means you can take crisp shots in tricky indoor and nighttime lighting.

            • Adobe Lightroom 5

              Enjoy advanced editing capabilities with the bundled Adobe Photoshop Lightroom 5 software. Use it to convert raw files and bring out all the details that match the moments to your memories.

            • Cloud storage

              Easily upload your photos to the cloud. The NX mini connects to Flickr and Dropbox and gives you 2GB of storage for free, so you can instantly access your memories from anywhere.

            • HD video recording

              The NX mini takes more than great photos. Shoot Full HD video at a whopping 30 frames per second with lifelike colour and extraordinarily sharp detail. Plus, the H.264 format lets you record for longer.

            A Closer Look

            • Slim design

              At 22.5mm, the NX mini is delightfully slim yet strong. It slides easily into purses and pockets alike.

            • Baby monitor

              Keep an eye on your sleeping baby from another room with the Home Monitor app on your smartphone. If your baby wakes up and cries, the NX mini sends you a notification.

            • More lenses, more freedom

              Elevate your NX mini’s performance with the NX M mount. The wide range of outstanding lenses means there’s a perfect lens for whatever you want to capture.

            • Touchscreen

              The NX mini features an intuitive touchscreen interface that makes it easier than ever to navigate.

            • Super-charged battery life

              Enough power to shoot the entire story. Every battery charge gives the NX mini enough power to take up to an incredible 650 shots and 190 minutes of video (9mm, CIPA Standard).1
