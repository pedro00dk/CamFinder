What's Included?

The Canon PIXMA TS8020 Wireless Inkjet All-In-One Printer is an exceptional everyday printer with features you just don't see every day. Its compact and innovative space-saving design allows it to be located just about anywhere in your home. Plus, the PIXMA TS8020 printer is available in four colors-black, white, red and brown-so it coordinates with just about any dcor. When it comes to printing connectivity, the TS8020 excels. Print beautiful photos or documents from any of your devices over your choice of Wi-Fi1, NFC2, USB, through the built-in SD card reader3 or even the Cloud. Intuitive features like a 4.3" touch screen and enhanced user interface, document removal reminder and auto expandable output tray make it a snap to use. It has a six-color ink system, including a dedicated gray ink tank, plus front-and-rear paper feeding options that even support fine art paper, so you never have to compromise on image quality. Great connectivity, great looks, and great-looking output - that's what you get with the Canon PIXMA TS8020 Wireless Inkjet All-In-One Printer.



  • Seamlessly print from you NFC Smartphone with PIXMA Touch & Print4.
  • Inspire your creativity with prints that will impress. From stunning photographs with Creative Filters to detailed documents put the Six Individual Ink System to work and never compromise on speed or quality.
  • With the Canon PRINT app7 conveniently print from your favorite mobile devices.
  • Versatile Paper Support with front and rear paper feeding ability.
  • With intuitive features like the 4.3" Touch LCD with its enhanced user interface, Document Removal Reminder, and an Auto-Expandable Output Tray, it's clear to see why the PIXMA TS8020 makes printing fast and easy.
  • Print from any room in the house with seamless Wi-Fi Support1. Enjoy the simplicity of connecting your smartphone, tablet and all your favorite devices with ease. Print hassle free - whether from the Cloud6, through NFC4, on social media7 or on the go.
  • The PIXMA TS8020 supports printing on multiple media types such as CD/DVD/Blu-ray Discs, Business Cards, 5"x5" Square paper and Fine Art Paper, so its ready for whatever you need to print.
