Not your typical binoculars, Vanguard's top-of-the-line Endeavor ED binoculars deliver sharpness and clarity like never before with exceptional light transmission and remarkable colour resolution and contrast. Other features include an ergonomic, open-bridge design for a comfortable fit, a wide view angle, BaK4 roof prisms, an anti-reflection coating, large focus adjustment wheel and long eye relief. Nitrogen-charged and o-ring sealed, the Endeavor ED binoculars are waterproof and fogproof to take on challenging weather conditions.

Key Features: Vanguard Endeavor ED 8x42 Binoculars

The magic behind its performance lies in the premium ED glass, which reduces colour dispersion to provide high resolution colours and clarity. The Endeavor ED Series has BaK4 roof prisms and special lens coatings for unbelievable quality. Nitrogen-charged and O-ring sealed, waterproof and fogproof to take on challenging weather conditions.

  • 42mm objective lenses

  • Open bridge design
