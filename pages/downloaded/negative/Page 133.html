K - Mount Lenses

  • Wide - Angle Lenses

    Wide angle of view and shooting distance beyond the human visual range for expression of distinctive photographic effects.

  • Standard Lenses

    Natural angle of view that is closest to the human visual range for providing images where the subject's attraction come through unaltered.

  • Telephoto Lenses

    Enlarges faraway subjects and brings them closer for dynamically capturing a specific area.

  • Macro Lenses

    Enables closeups of the miniature world of flowers and other small objects for capturing features in sharp, vivid detail.
