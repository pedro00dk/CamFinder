Designed to fit in the Bowens Gemini range between the 400 and the 500R lights, the Bowens Gemini Classic 500 Twin Head Kit is a reworking of the best-selling Gemini 500. Utilising the same twin dual-dial analogue control with the added benefits of a faster flash duration (1/900th compared with 1/700th), the option of adding radio trigger cards as used in the R and Pro series (sold separately), Auto Dump, and improved styling. This twin head kit includes two Gemini 500C heads, two umbrellas, two modelling lamps, two mains leads, and a sync lead all packaged in a newly designed case. The case is constructed from strong canvas and features an extendable handle, tough multi-terrain wheels, a heavy-duty zip, and custom-cut protective foam inserts that make it ideal for the photographer on the move.
