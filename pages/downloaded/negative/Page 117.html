        Perfect for portraiture

        The Canon EF 50 mm f/1.8 STM Standard Prime Lens offers a perspective that's similar to the human eye, making it ideal for portraiture and easy to achieve perfect framing with a smooth bokeh.

        Near-silent STM focusing eliminates focus noise â ideal when shooting movies.

        f/1.8 aperture

        A bright maximum aperture of f/1.8 delivers great results in low-light conditions, while also letting you capture a smooth background to make your subject stand out.

        Quality elements and coating

        This fixed lens offers sharp images with increased contrast and reduced distortion. The glass elements are layered with Canon's bespoke Super Spectra coating to help prevent flare and ghosting caused by shooting into the light.

        Travel friendly lens
